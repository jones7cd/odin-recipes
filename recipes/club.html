Club Sandwich

Learn how to make the best Club Sanwich!

While it may seem like a simple sandwich, there is a few key parts to this recipe that will take your Club Sandwich to the next level.

Ingredients

  • Turkey
  • Ham
  • Cheese (American Recommended)
  • Tomatoes
  • lettuce
  • Mayo
  • White Bread

Instructions

  1. Place your bacon on a baking sheet with parchment paper, and place in the oven
  2. AFTER the bacon is in the oven, turn it on to 400 F
  3. After 20 minutes, or when bacon is done to your liking, take out of oven and place on a plate with paper towels
  4. Toast white bread
  5. Spread mayo on toast
  6. Layer ham and turkey on bread
  7. Add tomato
  8. Add lettuce
  9. Add Bacon
  10. Add top piece of bread and cut in hald
