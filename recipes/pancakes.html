Pancakes

Learn how to make the best Bacon Egg & Cheese!

While it may seem like a simple meal, there is a few key parts to this recipe that will take your Pancakes to the next level.

Ingredients

  • Flour/li>
  • Eggs
  • Buttermilk
  • Butter
  • Chocolate Chips
  • Maple Syrup

Instructions

  1. Place your flour in a bowl with eggs and buttermilk
  2. Mix until batter is smooth with no chunks left in it
  3. Heat a pan with butter over medium heat
  4. Pour a cup of the batter into the pan
  5. Once the outer rin starts bubbling, it is time to flip
  6. Add chocolate chips
  7. Pancake is done when you stick a toothpick in and no batter is on the toothpick
  8. Cover in maple syrup and enjoy!
